================================================================================
  AMERICAN DREAM MEGA-MALL — INTERACTIVE SALES DECK
  Implementation Plan — Phase 1
================================================================================

PROJECT OVERVIEW
----------------
Client:        American Dream (East Rutherford, NJ)
Type:          Browser-Based Interactive Sales Deck
Purpose:       Close leasing, sponsorship, and event booking deals
Audience:      Prospective tenants, brand partners, event promoters

TECH STACK
----------
Framework:     React 18 + TypeScript (Vite)
Styling:       Vanilla CSS (CSS Modules) — NO Tailwind
Animations:    Framer Motion (scroll-triggered, cinematic transitions)
Routing:       React Router DOM (supports Phase 2 expandability)
Icons:         Lucide React
Fonts:         Google Fonts — Cormorant Garamond (luxury headings) + Inter (body)
Media:         Real-world YouTube embeds + Pexels free-use video URLs
AI Assets:     AI-generated imagery via Gemini image generation tool

DESIGN DIRECTION
----------------
Tone:          Modern. Confident. High-energy. Cinematic.
Color Palette:
  - Background:  #0a0a0a (near black)
  - Accent Gold: #c9a84c (luxury warm gold)
  - White:       #f5f5f0 (off-white)
  - Surface:     #141414
Aesthetic:     Dark luxury (inspired by Apple.com, Tesla, Louis Vuitton)
Typography:    Serif for display, Sans-serif for body — tight letter-spacing
Motion:        Scroll-triggered reveals, parallax video, number count-ups

PHASE 1 — SECTION STRUCTURE
----------------------------

SECTION 1: CINEMATIC HERO
  - Full-screen autoplay muted background video (American Dream promo footage)
  - Overlay: "NOT JUST A DESTINATION. A NEW STANDARD."
  - Scroll indicator + non-linear nav overlay (top navigation)
  - Fade-in animation on load

SECTION 2: WHY AMERICAN DREAM (The Scale)
  - Location context: 14 miles from NYC, New Jersey Metro
  - Interactive animated stat counters:
      → 3.5M sq ft  (Largest mall in North America)
      → 40M+ visitors/year
      → 450+ stores & restaurants
      → 16 major attractions
  - Map visual: NY/NJ catchment area
  - Scroll-triggered reveal animations

SECTION 3: THE AVENUE (Luxury Retail)
  - Dedicated luxury wing showcase
  - Tenants: Saks Fifth Avenue, Louis Vuitton, Hermès, Dior, Gucci, etc.
  - Aesthetic: All-white, editorial photography style
  - Sub-text: "The only luxury retail destination with a theme park next door"

SECTION 4: DINING & LIFESTYLE
  - Food hall + dining highlights
  - Real imagery/video of the dining experience
  - Narrative: "Over 100 dining options — from fast casual to fine dining"
  - Animated grid of dining highlights

SECTION 5: ATTRACTIONS & ENTERTAINMENT
  - Showcases the mega-attractions:
      → Big SNOW (North America's only indoor real snow ski slope)
      → Nickelodeon Universe (largest indoor theme park in North America)
      → DreamWorks Water Park (largest indoor water park in North America)
      → Legoland Discovery Center
      → SEA LIFE Aquarium
  - Horizontal scroll or carousel with video previews
  - Differentiator headline: "Where else can you ski and ride a roller coaster
    in the same afternoon?"

SECTION 6: EVENTS & THE PLATFORM
  - Concert hall / activation space showcase
  - Key data: event capacity, footfall during events, brand activation ROI
  - Real examples: major events hosted at American Dream
  - Position as: "A global platform, not just a building"
  - CTA: "Plan Your Activation"

SECTION 7: CALL TO ACTION (The Deal Closer)
  - Three distinct paths:
      → Button 1: "Explore Leasing Opportunities"
      → Button 2: "Discuss Brand Partnerships"
      → Button 3: "Book an Event"
  - Contact form (modal or inline)
  - Dark, cinematic background — bold, inviting design

NON-LINEAR NAVIGATION
---------------------
- Sticky top nav with section labels
- Clicking any nav item smooth-scrolls to that section
- Active section highlighted in nav
- Mobile hamburger menu (responsive)

PERFORMANCE STRATEGY
--------------------
- Lazy loading for all images and videos
- Intersection Observer API for scroll-triggered animations
- Code-split routes for Phase 2 modules
- Optimized assets: WebP images, compressed video
- Target: 90+ Lighthouse Score
